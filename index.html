INTRODUCTION

Five years later…

After a fatal journey through the post-pandemic United States, Ellie and Joel settle in Wyoming.

Living in a thriving community gives them some stability, despite the threat posed by the infected and desperate survivors.

When a violent incident disturbs this peace, Ellie begins a long and relentless quest for justice. As she hunts down those responsible, she finds herself confronted with the devastating physical and emotional consequences of her actions.

• Discover an emotional story in which your ideas of right and wrong will be tested.
• Venture into a world as dangerous as it is beautiful, traverse peaceful mountains and explore overgrown ruins.
• Enjoy a gameplay experience that combines action and survival, intense close quarters combat, smooth movement and dynamic stealth.
